Spaceteam is the one game on this list that requires all your friends to be in the same physical space.

In Spaceteam, you and your friends are the crew of a spaceship and everything's going wrong.

To stay on course, you'll need to work together by shouting different made-up space commands to each other.

The speed and the chaos ramp up and up, until you all inevitably crash and die, only to be awarded medals posthumously. But it's fun while it lasts.

If you have a smaller friend group, you can also try out the best two-player mobile games. Download: Spaceteam for iOS | Android (Free, in-app purchases available)
